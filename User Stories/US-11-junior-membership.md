# US-11 — Junior Membership Information

**Role:** Parent of a child (10+ years old)
**Goal:** Find information about junior membership and youth training
**Benefit:** Determine whether the club is suitable for their child before committing

## Story

> As a parent, I want to find information about junior membership and training so that I can determine whether my child can join.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Minimum age (10 years) is clearly stated on both the trial program and membership pages
- [ ] Junior membership fee (550 kr/år) is clearly separated from senior fee
- [ ] Training times suitable for juniors are indicated in the schedule
- [ ] It is clear that parents are welcome during the initial trial sessions
- [ ] The page explains that all equipment is provided (no upfront gear purchase needed)
- [ ] Family discount information is visible (relevant for siblings joining together)
- [ ] Contact email is easily accessible for follow-up questions

## Notes

- Current trial program: first Monday of each month, 18:00 (parents encouraged to attend with juniors)
- CMS editors can update age limits, prices, and schedule without developer help
