# US-07 — View Membership Fees

**Role:** Prospective member
**Goal:** Understand fee structure before signing up
**Benefit:** No need to email the club for pricing information

## Story

> As a prospective member, I want to see a clear breakdown of membership fees for seniors, juniors, and families so that I can understand the cost before signing up.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Fee table clearly shows: Senior (750 kr/år), Junior (550 kr/år), Stöd (100 kr/år)
- [ ] Family discount rules are explained in plain Swedish
- [ ] Swish number and bank giro are visible for easy payment
- [ ] The page explains that trial session fee (100 kr) is deducted from membership
- [ ] Pro-rated membership for joiners after September 1 is mentioned
- [ ] Wellness benefit (friskvård) information is present for employed members
- [ ] No login required to view this page

## Notes

- All fee data is CMS-editable (treasurer can update without developer)
